Sugar Cookies

Description

The perfect treat! Easy to make and quick to serve. add your own toppings like raisins or chocolate chips for a personalised touch!

Prep time: 20 mins
Additional time: 10 mins
servings: 48


Ingredients

  • 2 & 3/4 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon of baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon of baking powder
  • 1 cup of softened butter
  • 1 & 1/2 cups of white sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 190°C (375°F).
  2. Stir flour, baking soda and baking powder together in a small bowl.
  3. Beat sugar and butter together in a large bowl with a mixer until smooth. Beat in egg and vanilla, gradually blending in the flour mixture. Roll dough into walnut-sized balls and place 2" apart onto ungreased baking sheets.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven until edges are golden (8 to 10 minutes). Cool on the baking sheets briefly before removing to a wire rack to cool completely.
