Tomato Mozzarella Salad

Description

A simple and tasty dish that can be made from ingredients you can grow in your own garden! There really is nothing better!


Prep time: 10 mins

Additional time: None

servings: 6


Ingredients

  • 3 large tomatoes, sliced
  • 8 ounces mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • 1/4 cup of olive oil
  • 1/4 cup of balsamic vinegar
  • 1/4 teaspoon of salf
  • 1/8 teaspoon of ground black pepper
  • 1/4 cup of minced fresh basil

Steps

  1. Place tomato and mozzarella slices in an alternating pattern circling the serving platter.
  2. Combine oil, balsamic vinegar, salt and pepper in a sealable jar; shake well.
  3. drizzle jar over tomatoes and mozzarella, finally sprinkle with basil
